step by step view

2016/06/27 Editor’s choice

baoyachi/StepView
_StepView - Step by step，just use stepviewIndicator，step indicator，flow indicator，timeline，order process，express status_github.com

중국어로만 되어 있는데, 활용하기 어렵지는 않습니다.
